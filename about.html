YouTiming is an information system that acts as a critical layer between the market price fluctuations and the Book of Change (BOC, 易經). The layer applies the BOC as the source of verses to expose the visibility and infuse a "semantic" meaning into the trend that will drive and shape the future.

By learning from the historical data, the pattern can be traced and retrieved. Thus, statistical references can be derived for mid/long-term market trading. The stock market is a very efficient discounting mechanism. YouTiming maps that mechanism into BOC and produces the prophecy-like forecast.
