YouTiming is a system that serves as a bridge between the market price fluctuations and the Book of Change (BOC, aka I-Ching 易經--probably the earliest and the most ancient Big Data Model as in today's terms). The system applies the BOC as the source of forecast and finds a timingly appropriate verse for the computed trend.

The stock market is a very efficient discounting mechanism. YouTiming maps that mechanism into BOC and gives you a future vision from the perspective of I-Ching.
